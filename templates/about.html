Note -- a project for amateur and professional musicians, who want to learn how to play new songs on different instruments or write their own intead!

To write a new song, click on the 'create' and fill up information about the song (and, of course, add text to it), after that click on the button 'Submit' and voila!

To find a new song, click on the logo and choose whether you want to find a specific song or a variety of them, but on the specific instrument. You also have opportunity to shorten your search results by selecting more filters.

Enjoy!
